    • About Byron Rice

    Byron Rice has been a medical biller since September 1999 and a business owner since late 2007. When his mother needed surgery, Byron filled in for the two weeks that she was out of the dermatology practice where she was employed. It was there that Byron was introduced to medical billing. It seemed a natural fit as Byron was always good with numbers as well as following rules, guidelines, policy, and procedures. Following his two-week stint at the dermatology office, Byron was hired at a local billing service where he worked for a few months until he was hired away by Northwest Chiropractic Center (now part of VIDA Integrated Health) in Kirkland. During the course of his employment at NWCC, Byron worked in all aspects of business including office management (training for), billing, credentialing, and hiring (assisted). In December 2007, Byron opened his own billing service and made the move to full-time business owner in May 2008. Since that time, Byron has specialized in the reimbursement and management of alternative medicine providers; chiropractic, acupuncture, naturopath, and massage. His extensive work experience includes not only billing, but practice management and credentialing. His clients know him as a stickler for the rules and he believes that all three parties (patient, provider, insurance company) involved in the medical reimbursement process need to fulfill their responsibilities.
